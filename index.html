Troll Maze

Welcome to the troll maze. How far can you descend befor the trolls get to you. Beware the deeper you go the more powerfull the trolls.

Use the arrow keys or the buttons below to move your character.

You can wrap round the maze by moving off one side to get to the other.

You can break down walls with three attacks(to attack move into wall).

Trolls can not wrap around but walls do not impede them.

The deeper you go the faster the trolls.

It's game over if you are caught by a troll.
